About CSReD

A center built for
meaningful research.

The Center for Software Research and Development (CSReD) was established to create a collaborative environment where researchers, engineers, and industry practitioners come together to tackle foundational and applied problems in software and computing.

We operate at the intersection of academic rigor and industry relevance — producing publications, open-source tools, prototypes, and consulting engagements that push the state of the art while delivering tangible value to our partners.

From foundational algorithms to enterprise-scale systems architecture, our work spans the full spectrum of software research.

What We Offer

Our
services

Beyond publishing research, CSReD offers a range of engagement models for organizations that want to leverage our expertise.

Discuss a Project
01Applied Research & R&D▲

We conduct sponsored research projects aligned with our core expertise areas, producing peer-reviewed outputs, prototypes, and intellectual property co-owned with partners.

  • Problem scoping and feasibility analysis
  • Experimental design and execution
  • Technical reports and publication support
  • Prototype development and handoff
02Technical Consulting▼

Our researchers provide expert advisory services to engineering teams, guiding architectural decisions, technology selection, and performance optimization with an evidence-based approach.

  • Architecture review and design guidance
  • Technology strategy and roadmapping
  • Code quality and process audits
03Software Development▼

We build production-grade software systems and tools, often arising from our own research output, including AI-powered applications, data platforms, and developer tooling.

  • Custom software design and implementation
  • AI/ML model productionization
  • Open-source tool maintenance and support
04Training & Workshops▼

We deliver intensive technical workshops and training programs for engineering teams, covering advanced topics in software architecture, AI systems, security, and data engineering.

  • On-site and remote workshop delivery
  • Custom curriculum development
  • Hands-on labs and guided exercises
05Security Assessment▼

Leveraging our cybersecurity research group, we offer comprehensive security assessments covering code, architecture, and operational practices — with formal reports and remediation guidance.

  • Penetration testing and vulnerability analysis
  • Formal threat modeling
  • Privacy compliance review
06Academic Partnerships▼

We collaborate with universities and research institutions on joint projects, grant applications, student supervision, and cross-institutional publication initiatives.

  • Joint grant writing and submission
  • Co-supervision of graduate students
  • Shared lab and dataset resources
